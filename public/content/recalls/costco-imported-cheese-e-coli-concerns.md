# Costco Pulls Imported Cheese Over E. coli Concerns

Costco Wholesale has voluntarily recalled Fromagerie du Mont-Blanc Artisan Raw Milk Brie after FDA import testing identified the presence of Shiga toxin-producing E. coli (STEC) in samples of the product.
