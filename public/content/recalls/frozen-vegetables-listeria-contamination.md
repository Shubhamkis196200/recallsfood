# FDA Alert: Popular Frozen Vegetables Contaminated with Listeria

The FDA announced a major recall of frozen vegetable products manufactured by FreshPak Foods, a leading private-label frozen food supplier. The recall affects products sold under multiple retail brands at major grocery chains nationwide.
