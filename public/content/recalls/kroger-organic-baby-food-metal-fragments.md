# Kroger Recalls Organic Baby Food After Metal Fragments Found

Kroger Co. has issued a voluntary recall of select Simple Truth Organic baby food pouches after three consumers in different states reported finding small metal fragments in the product. The FDA has classified this as a Class I recall due to the potential for serious injury.
