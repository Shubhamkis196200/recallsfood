# National Recall: Peanut Butter Brand Tests Positive for Aflatoxin

NuttyBest Foods Inc. has issued a nationwide voluntary recall of its creamy and crunchy peanut butter products after internal quality testing revealed aflatoxin levels above the FDA action level of 20 parts per billion.
