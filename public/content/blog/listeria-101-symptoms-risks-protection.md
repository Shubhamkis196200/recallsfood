# Listeria 101: Symptoms, Risks, and How to Protect Your Family

Listeria monocytogenes is one of the deadliest foodborne pathogens, with a fatality rate of 20-30% among high-risk groups. Unlike most bacteria, Listeria can grow at refrigerator temperatures, making it particularly dangerous. This guide covers symptoms to watch for, who is most at risk, and practical steps to protect your family.
