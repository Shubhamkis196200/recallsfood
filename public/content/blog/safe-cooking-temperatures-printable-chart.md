# Safe Cooking Temperatures for Every Type of Meat (Printable Chart)

Cooking meat to the correct internal temperature is the most reliable way to kill harmful bacteria. The USDA recommends specific minimum temperatures for different types of meat: 165°F for poultry, 160°F for ground meats, and 145°F for whole cuts of beef, pork, lamb, and veal. Use this printable chart as a quick reference in your kitchen.
