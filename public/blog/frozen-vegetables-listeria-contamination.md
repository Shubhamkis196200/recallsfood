# FDA Alert: Popular Frozen Vegetables Contaminated with Listeria

The FDA announced a major recall of frozen vegetable products manufactured by FreshPak Foods. The recall affects products sold under multiple retail brands at major grocery chains nationwide. Listeria monocytogenes was detected during routine FDA sampling.

## What To Do

1. Check your freezer for any frozen vegetable products from the listed brands
2. Do not consume products with matching lot numbers, even if cooked
3. Clean and sanitize any surfaces that may have contacted the products
