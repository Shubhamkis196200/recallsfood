# Kroger Recalls Organic Baby Food Over Metal Fragments

Kroger Co. has issued a voluntary recall of select Simple Truth Organic baby food pouches after three consumers reported finding small metal fragments in the product. The FDA classified this as a Class I recall.

## What To Do

1. Do not feed these products to your child
2. Return products to any Kroger family store for a full refund
3. If your child has consumed these products and shows signs of distress, seek medical attention immediately
