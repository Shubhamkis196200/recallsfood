# How to Store Food Safely: Refrigerator Temperature Guide

Bacteria multiply rapidly between 40°F and 140°F (the "Danger Zone"). Keep your refrigerator at 40°F or below and freezer at 0°F or below. Use a separate appliance thermometer — don't trust the built-in dial.
