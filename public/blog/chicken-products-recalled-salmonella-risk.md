# Urgent: Chicken Products Recalled Due to Salmonella Risk

**Over 4.2 million pounds of frozen chicken recalled across 38 states**

The USDA's Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) announced a Class I recall affecting approximately 4.2 million pounds of frozen chicken products manufactured by Heritage Farms. The recall was initiated after routine FSIS testing detected Salmonella Enteritidis in samples collected from retail locations.

## What To Do

1. Stop consuming these products immediately
2. Check your freezer for matching lot numbers and UPC codes
3. Return products to your place of purchase for a full refund
4. If you have consumed these products and feel ill, contact your healthcare provider
5. Report adverse reactions to the USDA at 1-888-674-6854

## Health Risks

Salmonella can cause serious and sometimes fatal infections in young children, elderly people, and those with weakened immune systems. Symptoms include diarrhea, fever, and abdominal cramps 12 to 72 hours after exposure.
