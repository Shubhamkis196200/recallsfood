# National Recall: Peanut Butter Brand Tests Positive for Aflatoxin

NuttyBest has recalled its popular peanut butter products after testing revealed aflatoxin levels exceeding FDA limits. Aflatoxins are naturally occurring toxins that pose long-term health risks including liver cancer.
