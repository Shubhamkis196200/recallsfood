# The Complete Guide to Food Thermometer Safety

A food thermometer is the only reliable way to ensure your food has been cooked to a safe temperature.

## Safe Minimum Internal Temperatures
- Poultry (whole, parts, ground): 165°F (74°C)
- Ground meats (beef, pork, lamb): 160°F (71°C)
- Beef, pork, lamb (steaks, roasts): 145°F (63°C) + 3-minute rest
- Fish and shellfish: 145°F (63°C)
- Leftovers and casseroles: 165°F (74°C)
