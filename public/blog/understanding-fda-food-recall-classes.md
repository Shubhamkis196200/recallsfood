# Understanding FDA Food Recall Classes: I, II, and III

## Class I: Dangerous or Defective
Most serious. Reasonable probability of serious adverse health consequences or death. Examples: Listeria, Salmonella, E. coli O157:H7, glass/metal in food.

## Class II: May Cause Temporary Health Problems
Moderate risk. Temporary or medically reversible health consequences. Examples: undeclared allergens, minor contamination.

## Class III: Not Likely to Cause Health Problems
Low risk. Minor labeling issues or cosmetic defects.
